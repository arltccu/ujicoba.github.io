History

  • Akademi Bahasa Asing or ABA Pontianak was founded in 1994 with D3 program of English Major
  • ABA Pontianak changed to Sekolah Tinggi Bahasa Asing or STBA Pontianak on October 17th 2014
  • STBA Pontianak officially had its S1 program of English Literature in 2015

    • STBA Pontianak was accredited "B" (Baik) BAN-PT in 2006 and 2014 consecutively.
